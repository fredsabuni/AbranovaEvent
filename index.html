Abraham N’ghwani

Entrepreneur, Investor and Scholar

CEO and Founder of Abranova Real Estate

Abraham Ng'hwani, a Reginaldo Howard Memorial Scholar, graduated with his Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering and Materials Science from the Pratt School of Engineering at Duke University with minors in Arabic, Chinese and French. Originally from Tanzania, Mr. Ng’hwani has used his extensive appreciation for international relations and engineering to propel his career in real estate development and investment.

His experience at Gilbane Building Company, a Fortune 500 construction company with an annual revenue of $4.5 billion, gave him a great platform to put his gained skills into practice like project management, budget efficiency, pre construction takeoffs, analysis, bid tabs and GMP documents. In 2017, Abraham decided to expand his brand with Abranova Real Estate, a full-stack real estate investment and construction development company.

His core mission is to democratize real estate development by providing investment opportunities to everyone. Abranova Real Estate uses technology integration like data analytics and simulations to develop their residential projects and has raised over $1.04MM for the first phase of construction. The project is Currently operating the project at a 104% budget efficiency and on-track to deliver the projected $2.5MM in revenues.

Background

Mr. Cohen co-oversees all fundraising efforts, acquisitions, dispositions and development at Urbium. Prior to co-founding Urbium, he worked at Thor Urbana where was directly involved in the acquisition and development of almost 12 million square feet of real estate, with a value of over $2 billion.

He also participated closely in all asset management, fundraising and investor relation efforts. Previously, he worked in investment positions at Apollo Real Estate Advisors, the Carlyle Group and Evercore Partners. In over 12 years in real estate, he has been involved in $3.5 billion of real estate transactions in Mexico, the United States, and Brazil in the residential, retail, office, hotel and mixed-use sectors.

Mr. Cohen is a Board Member of the Urban Land Institute where he was awarded a Rising Star Award and serves as a member of the Board of Trustees for an Orphanage in Mexico. He graduated summa cum laude from Manhattan College and received his MBA from the Wharton School at the University of Pennsylvania where he was a Bucksbaum Fellow.

Real Estate Titans By Erez Cohen

This novel is For beginners and people just entering the real estate market who want to learn from the best in the world.
